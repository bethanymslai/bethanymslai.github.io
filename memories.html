My Contributions

memories is a dreamy, pastel-toned fusion of dress-up game and visual novel, designed to evoke a sense of longing, softness, and emotional resonance. My goal was to create an experience that merged nostalgic genres in a way that felt personal and intimate, capturing the quiet sadness of remembering something beautiful that’s now gone.

Originally developed in 24 hours as a self-imposed challenge, I focused early on building a modular scene system and a custom dialogue tag parser to streamline iteration. This let me spend the bulk of my time on what mattered most: storyboarding, rewriting the narrative for emotional clarity, and illustrating the game’s hand-drawn visuals to reflect its quiet tone.

As my debut release, I didn’t expect memories to strike such a chord, but its heartfelt reception on Steam, with 95% Overwhelmingly Positive reviews and over 60K downloads, was deeply encouraging. Seeing players share how the story moved them remains one of my proudest moments, and I hope to continue crafting games that offer that same emotional connection.
