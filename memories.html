Steam page

My Contributions

Merging the genres of dress-up game and visual novel, memories highlights the sadness and nostalgia in an immersive, pastel experience.

Initially created in 24 hours as a self-challenge, I focused on architecting a tag system and modular scene ordering for easy iteration so I could dedicate the majority of my time towards storyboarding, script-revising, and illustrating all the hand-drawn art assets required for an intimate, dreamy experience.

As memories was my debut project, I was pleasantly surprised at its overwhelmingly positive reception on Steam, and it warmed my heart to see how many players were moved by my game's story. I hope to continue releasing games that touch people's hearts the way memories has.

Tag system to auto-format dialogue

Architected a tag system to auto-format dialogue text and allow for easy and quick narrative iteration. The system contains unique tags for different speakers & dialogue choices.

Dynamic scene ordering

Using custom story data scriptable objects, different story scenes can be easily reordered to iterate on story beats.

100% hand-drawn art assets

Illustrated and implemented 70+ art assets in 24 hours with a dreamy art-style, including entire story scenes, Steam promo assets, and 5 brand new illustrations for a fan-pack.
