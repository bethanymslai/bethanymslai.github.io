Duckbound

Developer (2025)

Duckbound is a light-hearted turn-based RPG with real-time parry mechanics. The player unlocks abilities and combos them to produce different slapstick effects that reflect their opponent's personality.

Created in 1 week for the 2025 Shovel Game Jam.

Relevant Tools: Unity, C#, GitHub

Itch page

My Contributions

Duckbound started with a simple idea: what if unlocking silly, small abilities could lead to meaningful moments? I shaped every part of the game, from the core concept and systems to making sure the mechanics fit the cozy, heartfelt story. The goal was to create a world that feels warm, expressive, and emotionally rich, all beginning with a little duck.

I built the gameâ€™s architecture to be fully modular. Using ScriptableObjects and custom editor tools, I made it easy to tweak and expand everything, from dialogue to enemy attack patterns. This allowed the team to prototype freely while staying true to the main vision. Characters, areas, and abilities grew together, making a tight, personality-filled experience.

During development, I led a remote team, guiding art and music direction, writing the full GDD, and handling implementation and polish. From juicy Osu!-style parry combat to dialogue full of character, Duckbound is designed to feel alive, full of humor, charm, and unexpected emotional depth.
