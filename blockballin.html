My Contributions

Inspired by Cut the Rope and Where's My Water?, our team of four set out to design a puzzle-platformer that blended dexterity with strategy. From the start, I ideated the core mechanic and helped shape our goal: to create meaningful, open-ended puzzles using a single mechanic, encouraging creativity and multiple solutions.

As producer, I managed task delegation, adjusted scope based on weekly playtests, and ensured smooth coordination across the team. I also helped structure systems to be modular, making it easy for team members to prototype, test, and iterate levels independently.

We carefully balanced fast reflex moments with slow, thoughtful planning, using built-in hints and pacing gates to guide players through handcrafted challenges.
