About Me

I'm Bethany, a game designer and engineer from Taiwan. I graduated from USC's School of Cinematic Arts with a degree in Interactive Entertainment and a minor in video game programming. The USC Games program is recognized as Princeton Review's #1 game design program in North America.

Previously I worked at Playbook XR as a software developer, where I architected and optimized core front-end features for VR and Desktop platforms. I also worked at Airstrafe Interactive as a gameplay programmer on their open-world survival craft game Saleblazers. Currently I work as a computer science teacher.

In my free time, I love to watch game design video essays, make VTuber models, and solve mysteries with Professor Layton on my 3DS, usually with a warm mocha at my local café.
